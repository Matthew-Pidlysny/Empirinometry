3. Hereafter and Accountability
4. Worship and Obedience

6. Natural Signs and Creation

- Makki Surahs: Focus on faith, creation, prophets
- Madani Surahs: Focus on laws, social issues, community
- Specific themes: praise, warning, guidance, stories
