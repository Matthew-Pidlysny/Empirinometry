## Basic Tajweed Rules

### Ghunnah (Nasal Sound)
- Letters: ن (Noon) and م (Meem) with shaddah
- Duration: 2 counts (harakat)
- Occurs in: إِنَّ, مِنْ, ثُمَّ
- Description: Nasal resonance sound produced through the nose

### Idgham (Merging)
- Types: Idgham with Ghunnah, Idgham without Ghunnah
- Letters: ن + (ي, م, و, ن) = with ghunnah
- Letters: ن + (ر, ل) = without ghunnah
- Example: مِن يَوْمِهِمْ (idgham with ghunnah)

### Ikhfa (Hiding)
- Letters: 15 letters after Noon Saakin or Tanween
- Ghunnah duration: 2 counts
- Example: أَنْبِئْهُمْ (ikhfa of noon)

### Iqlab (Conversion)
- Occurs when Noon Saakin/Tanween is followed by ب
- Noon sound changes to Meem sound
- Example: مِنْ بَعْدِ (noon becomes meem)

### Madd (Elongation)
- Natural Madd: 2 counts
- Connected Madd: 4-5 counts
- Separated Madd: 4-5 counts
- Compulsory Madd: 6 counts
- Letters: ا, و, ي

### Qalqalah (Bouncing)
- Letters: ق, ط, ب, ج, د
- Stronger when saakin, weaker when with shaddah
- Example: يَطْمَعُونَ, يَبْصُطُونَ

## Advanced Tajweed Concepts

### Heavy and Light Letters
- Heavy (Tafkhim): خ, غ, ص, ض, ط, ظ, ق, ر (when specific conditions)
- Light (Tarqeeq): remaining letters
- Depends on vowel and position in word

### Makharij (Articulation Points)
- 17 main articulation points
- From throat to lips
- Proper placement affects sound quality

### Sifaat (Attributes)
- Essential attributes: sifat lazim (permanent)
- Temporary attributes: sifat 'arid (temporary)
- Combination creates unique letter sounds

## Common Tajweed Patterns in Quran

### Bismillah Patterns
- بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
- Multiple tajweed rules in one phrase
- Ikhfa in بِسْمِ, idgham in الرَّحْمَٰنِ

### Common Verse Endings
- Patterns of qalqalah
- Madd at verse endings
- Ghunnah variations

## Educational Notes

### Learning Sequence
1. Master Arabic alphabet pronunciation
2. Learn makharij (articulation points)
3. Practice sifaat (attributes)
4. Study basic tajweed rules
5. Apply to Quran recitation

### Common Mistakes
- Incorrect makharij
- Wrong ghunnah duration
- Missing madd requirements
- Improper qalqalah execution

### Practice Tips
- Start with short surahs
- Record and evaluate own recitation
- Learn from qualified teachers
- Practice regularly with patience
